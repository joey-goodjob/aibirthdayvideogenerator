Can I create a video from a photo?

Yes! You can upload a photo as an optional starting point. The AI uses it to make your video feel more personal. If you prefer, you can skip the photo and create from text alone.

How long are the videos?

Our videos are 5-12 seconds long—perfect for messaging apps, social media stories, and quick birthday greetings that are easy to watch and share.

Do I need video editing skills?

Not at all! The tool is designed for people who don't want to learn complex editing software. Just write your birthday message, choose a style, and generate.

Can I edit the video after generation?

We focus on generation, not editing. If you want to add subtitles, trim, or make other edits, you can download the video and use tools like CapCut, Canva, or Premiere.

How do I control the video style?

Describe what you want in plain words: 'warm and cozy', 'fun and playful', 'elegant and minimal'. The AI interprets your description and creates a matching video.
